About Project

Built for accessible, joyful learning

🎯

Problem

Many students need quick revision support, but paid tools, complex logins, and internet dependency can make learning unequal.

💡

Solution

Knowledge Hub AI offers notes, quiz practice, and doubt support as a free offline browser demo using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

🚀

Innovation

It simulates AI-style learning assistance with local templates and rules, making the idea easy to demonstrate without API costs.

🌍

Impact

The project supports SDG 4 by promoting inclusive, equitable, and quality education for school students.

Target users: School students who want simple, friendly, no-cost study support.
